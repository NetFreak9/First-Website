Hi!

Have you ever worried about the problem of unemploymrnt? Maybe you never felt the need to. Just imagine a person having earned a decent degree after studying and spending sleepless night but he doesn't get placed anywhere. Imagine the pressure he and his family goes through. Well this is the cause of worry at a lot of homes these days.

One of the major problems these days is unemployment, especially in rural areas. Due to lack of opportunities people from the villages are migrating to cities in search of jobs which leads to congestion and overcrowding in urban areas. Hence, it is necessary to generate new employment opportunities for the rural people.

How does unemployment affect us?

Got placed and earning a satisfing salary? Unemployment would still haunt you.How?

Unemployment breeds many social problems comprising of dishonesty, gambling, bribery, theft etc.

Unemployment gives birth to political instability in country. Unemployed persons can easily be enticed by antisocial elements. They lose all faith in democratic values and peaceful means.

It has resulted in increase in crime rates. Quite often the unemployed youth commit crimes to earn their living.

As a consequence of lack opportunities in India, intelligent youth migrate to foreign countries. Their intellectual abilities are used by foreign countries which causes loss to India.

Unemployed people contribute less to the economy because they are spending less. They do not participate in paying tax due to lack of jobs.Thus it affects our economy also.
